# MoodleGrab — Chrome Web Store Listing

## Name

MoodleGrab

## Short Description

Download all your Moodle course files in one click — bulk download, ZIP packaging, and smart file management.

## Detailed Description

**MoodleGrab — Download all your Moodle course files in one click**

Tired of clicking through dozens of Moodle pages to download lecture slides, assignments, and readings one by one? MoodleGrab lets you grab everything at once.

**How it works**

1. Navigate to any Moodle course page
2. MoodleGrab automatically detects the site and scans for downloadable files
3. Browse, filter, and select the files you need
4. Hit download — done

**Key Features**

**Auto-Detection**
MoodleGrab automatically recognizes when you're on a Moodle site. It works with Moodle 3.x and 4.x across universities and institutions worldwide. No configuration needed — just install and go.

**File Browser with Tree View**
See every file in your course organized by section and activity. A familiar tree structure with checkboxes lets you select exactly what you want — pick individual files, entire sections, or everything at once. File sizes and type icons help you decide what to grab.

**Smart Filters**
Quickly narrow down files by type: Documents, Images, Videos, Audio, or Archives. Need only PDFs and PowerPoints? Set a custom filter. You can also skip oversized files with a size filter.

**Bulk Download with ZIP Packaging**
Selected files are packaged into a neatly organized ZIP file. Inside, files are sorted into folders by course section, so everything stays organized on your computer. Duplicate filenames are handled automatically.

**Concurrent Downloads**
MoodleGrab downloads multiple files in parallel for maximum speed. Configure the concurrency level (1–6 simultaneous downloads) to match your connection. A progress bar with ETA keeps you informed.

**Download Queue**
Pause, resume, or cancel downloads at any time. Failed downloads are automatically retried. The queue runs in the background, so you can keep browsing while files download.

**Download History**
Keep track of what you've downloaded. MoodleGrab logs every download session with course name, file count, total size, and date. Re-download previous bundles with one click.

**What's New Detection**
Revisiting a course? MoodleGrab highlights files that have been added since your last download. Grab only the new stuff with a single click — no need to re-download everything.

**Dark Mode**
Follows your system preference or toggle it manually. Easy on the eyes during late-night study sessions.

**Side Panel Interface**
MoodleGrab opens in Chrome's side panel, giving you plenty of space to browse files while keeping the Moodle page visible. No tiny popups.

**Privacy First**
MoodleGrab collects zero data. No analytics, no tracking, no accounts. Your settings and download history are stored locally on your device and never leave your browser.

**Who is this for?**

MoodleGrab is built for university and college students who use Moodle LMS. Whether you're downloading an entire semester's worth of lecture slides before exams or grabbing this week's readings, MoodleGrab saves you time and clicks.

**Permissions**

MoodleGrab only requests the minimum permissions needed to function:
- Access to the active tab to scan for files on Moodle pages
- Storage to save your settings and download history locally
- Downloads to save files to your computer

No broad website access. No data collection. Just a tool that does one thing well.
